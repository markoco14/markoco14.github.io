About WBA

The Whiteboard Art Store was founded by Teacher Mark in early 2021. Here at WBA we love teaching, and we believe that clear, funny whiteboard art is the best way to get the point across. We want teachers to share their whiteboards with the world and improve the education of people from all walks of life. No matter your background or income level, you should be able to see and understand the world. With that goal in mind, WBA was born.
